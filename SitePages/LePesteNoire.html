Combat Design

The goal was to emulate Vindictus-style combat, focusing on weight-based and fluid slashing motions. With the help of my fantastic team, our goal was to create a large world like Elden Ring, with the combo strings of Monster Hunter and the fluidity of Vindictus. This allowed a more fluid combat experiance and with camera motion, vfx, and audio to enchance the gameplay.

Knight Enemies

My role as gameplay programmer was to implement a majority of enemies. The goal was for a varity of medival knights with a wide spread arsenal. This caused the player to be constanly aware of their surroundings.

  • Designed and developed actors and behavior trees
  • Implemented blocking, combos, charge attacks, and patrolling features in C++
  • Created customizable weapons, enabling each enemy to have different weapons

Kraken Boss

My role as gameplay programmer to program the first boss in the game. The goal was for a defencive boss that fires projectiles. As the player gets closer the boss responces with a varity of attacks. This was to provide a back and forth gameplay loop for the player.

  • Designed and developed boss and behavior trees
  • Designed projectile attacks to push players and large sweeping attacks in C++
  • Created guard breaking system that stuns bosses based on the strength of the players attack
