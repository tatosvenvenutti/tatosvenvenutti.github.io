DirectX 11 Renderer

This project posed a significant challenge as I had no prior experience with graphics. Despite this, I opted for DirectX11 due to its user-friendly nature. The process began with creating a base scene in Blender, which I then integrated into the project. I organized the data using an Object-Oriented approach. I then implemented transformation matrices and expanded the project with additional features such as music and multiple levels.
