Game Of Life

This project aimed to showcase Conway's Game of Life implementation and visualization, marking my initial foray into basic artificial intelligence concepts. It also served as a platform to hone my UI design skills, leading me to acquire a wealth of new knowledge and abilities in the process.

3D Render

This project posed a significant challenge as I had no prior experience with graphics. Despite this, I opted for DirectX11 due to its user-friendly nature. The process began with creating a foundational map in Blender, which I then integrated into the project. I organized the data using an Object-Oriented approach. Subsequently, I incorporated matrices and expanded the project with features like music and diverse levels.
